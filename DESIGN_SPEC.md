# 📐 COGNOS Portfolio: Design & Brand Specification

## 1. Professional Identity (The Architect)
- **Primary Title:** AI Systems Architect
- **Alternative:** Principal Agentic Architect
- **Rationale:** Connects architectural roots (structural logic, foresight) with current work in agentic AI and automated infrastructure for high-stakes finance (REITs) and compliance.
- **Sub-headline:** "Engineering the convergence of structural logic, multi-billion baht asset management, and agentic AI stacks."

## 2. Aesthetic Direction (Brutalist Tech)
- **Concept:** Terminal-Minimalism / Structural Expressionism.
- **Philosophy:** "Truth to Materials." Reveal the logic, the grids, and the data rather than hiding them behind decorative layers.
- **Background:** `#0B0F19` (Deep Obsidian Blue-Black) with subtle gradients for a "secret vibe."
- **Primary Accent:** `#00FF66` (Terminal Neo-Green) — representing the active logic flow.
- **Secondary Accent:** `#FF3E55` (System Alert Red) — representing critical safety/compliance thresholds.
- **Typography:**
  - **Data Layer:** Crisp Monospace (Reference: IBM Plex Mono, JetBrains Mono).
  - **Structural Layer:** Sharp, structural Sans-Serif (Reference: Inter, Roboto, or Outfit).

## 3. Case Studies (The Q3 Pillars)

### Case Study 1: Enterprise Automation — The 10-Minute Reconciliation Engine
*   **The Problem:** Legacy, manual commission fee reconciliation processes consuming 10 hours of high-cognitive load labor per cycle, prone to human error.
*   **The Architecture:** Designed and deployed a hybrid VBA algorithmic engine integrated with Microsoft Copilot workflows, refactoring the data-ingestion and validation pipeline.
*   **The Impact:** Compressed processing time from 10 hours to 10 minutes (a 98.3% efficiency gain) while maintaining a 99% accuracy threshold. Selected as the sole platform presenter to showcase this operational optimization at the 2024 SCB Digital Copilot Showcase.

### Case Study 2: Asset Expansion — The Green Park 1 Financial Orchestration
*   **The Problem:** Navigating a highly volatile and competitive Capital Market environment to secure complex institutional and retail investor consensus for a major capital raise.
*   **The Architecture:** Engineered the end-to-end financial feasibility models, risk-mitigation frameworks, and structured the investor communication interface for the Green Park 1 Additional Investment (December 2025).
*   **The Impact:** Successfully secured absolute Trust unitholder approval, strategically expanding the REIT’s asset base and demonstrating proof-of-concept for data-driven investor alignment under market stress.

### Case Study 3: Legacy Refactoring — The SIRIP to SIRIPRT Structural Conversion
*   **The Problem:** Executing a highly complex structural migration of a legacy Property Fund (SIRIP) into a modern Real Estate Investment Trust (SIRIPRT) without disrupting asset continuity or violating strict compliance boundaries.
*   **The Architecture:** Acted as the Lead Systems & Property Fund Manager, architecting the end-to-end operational, legal, and regulatory blueprint. Coordinated a multi-disciplinary network including the SEC, specialized legal counsel, and asset management teams.
*   **The Impact:** Successfully executed one of SCBAM’s most structurally intricate fund transformations, establishing a scalable blueprint for legacy-to-modern asset refactoring.
